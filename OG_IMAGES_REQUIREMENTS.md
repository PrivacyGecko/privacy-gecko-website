# Open Graph Images Requirements for Privacy Gecko

**CRITICAL**: Token launch November 2025 - og-token.png is highest priority!

## Overview
Need 9 Open Graph images for social media sharing (Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.)

## Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions**: 1200x630px (exact)
- **File Format**: PNG (primary), WebP (optional for optimization)
- **File Size**: <100KB per image (aim for 50-80KB)
- **Color Mode**: RGB
- **Location**: `/public/images/`

## Design Requirements

### Brand Guidelines
- **Primary Color**: gecko-green (#00D98A)
- **Logo**: Privacy Gecko logo (available in `/public/images/`)
- **Typography**: Clean, modern, readable at small sizes
- **Style**: Privacy-focused, professional yet approachable
- **Consistency**: All images should feel like part of same family

### Safe Zones
- **Text**: Keep important text 80px from edges (Twitter/Facebook crop)
- **Logo**: Position in corner with 40px margin
- **Key Visual**: Center important elements

## Design Tips
1. **Readable at Small Sizes**: Text should be legible when image is 400px wide
2. **High Contrast**: Ensure text stands out from background
3. **Mobile Preview**: Test how image looks on mobile social feeds
4. **Brand Consistency**: Use Privacy Gecko brand colors and typography
5. **Avoid Text Overload**: Maximum 3 lines of text per image
6. **Visual Hierarchy**: Most important element should be largest/central
7. **Privacy Aesthetic**: Use shield, lock, encryption, anonymity visuals consistently
